We are a Christian based organization that is focused on equipping computer skills to people in less advantaged communities. Our lessons are completely free of charge and can be attended by anyone. We believe in a bright future, but it can only be bright if we work together on making it so. Our aim is to make sure our communities have the highly needed computer skills in order to be able to traverse this world that is turning digital on a fast pace.
